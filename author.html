ABOUT ME

BLESSED BY THE CREATION OF GOD

Lord,when you created nature you have purpose in every details you put on it.The light of your sun that waking me up every morning tells me that I am very blessed to have another life.Your warm and fresh air,reminds me that for all the problems and challenges I am facing today I have to breath for awhile because I have you,with you nothing is impossible.Trees,you will be there to protect me when no one else left in me.Rainbow,reminds me that I have to be patient,work hard,and stay humble because all the sacrifices has great endings.Your birds in the sky,tells me that life is beautiful,do whatever your heart wants because we only live once.Rain,it reminds me of all the blessings I received from you.I only have to TRUST and have FAITH in you.I am truly blessed to experience the beauty of your creation.NATURE,you are wonderful.
